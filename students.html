Student Interviews: Reflections on Fast Fashion

What would make you shop more sustainably?

  • "Knowing that workers in developing countries wouldn’t be worse off (maybe with some sort of subsidy). Right now, if your goal is to boycott those stores, not only is EPS too small of a community to make any real difference, even if more people did so, they would feel better about having some something good for the environment when in reality they would have just contributed to taking someone’s job and pushing them into even worse poverty."
